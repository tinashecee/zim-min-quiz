Rule 1

The quiz game consists of 4 rounds with 10 questions per round

Rule 2

Inorder to advance to the next round you much get atleast 5 out of 10 questions correct

Rule 3

If you fail to advance to the next round you are eliminated

Rule 4

You are given only 100 seconds to answer each question in the rounds. If the time runs out you will automatically be disqualified from the quiz game.
